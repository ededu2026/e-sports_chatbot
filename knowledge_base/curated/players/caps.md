# Caps

- Category: player
- Game: League of Legends
- Aliases: Caps, caps
- Full name: Rasmus Winther
- Nationality: Danish
- Roles: mid laner
- Known for: elite laning, creativity, clutch international performances

Caps is a Danish professional League of Legends player best known as one of the greatest Western mid laners in LoL esports history. He is especially associated with G2 Esports and with deep international tournament runs.
