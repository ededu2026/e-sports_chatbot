# FalleN

- Category: player
- Game: Counter-Strike
- Aliases: FalleN, fallen
- Full name: Gabriel Toledo
- Nationality: Brazilian
- Roles: AWPer, in-game leader
- Known for: one of the most iconic Brazilian Counter-Strike players, leadership, AWPing, championship legacy

Gabriel Toledo, known as `FalleN`, is a Brazilian Counter-Strike professional and one of the most important figures in the history of Brazilian esports. He became globally known for elite AWPing, in-game leadership, and for helping build championship-winning Brazilian lineups in Counter-Strike.
