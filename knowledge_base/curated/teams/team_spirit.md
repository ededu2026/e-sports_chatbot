# Team Spirit

- Category: team
- Primary games: Counter-Strike 2, Dota 2, PUBG Mobile, Mobile Legends: Bang Bang
- Region: Europe
- Base: Belgrade
- Founded: 2015

Team Spirit is a major esports organization with top-level presence across multiple titles. In Counter-Strike 2, the organization is strongly associated with star players such as donk and has become one of the most recognizable brands in modern esports competition.
