# Natus Vincere

- Category: team
- Aliases: Natus Vincere, NAVI, NaVi
- Primary games: Counter-Strike 2, Dota 2
- Region: Europe
- Notable association: s1mple, Aleksib, b1t

Natus Vincere, often called NAVI, is one of the most iconic organizations in Counter-Strike history. The brand has long been associated with superstar talent, championship contention, and some of the biggest names in esports.
